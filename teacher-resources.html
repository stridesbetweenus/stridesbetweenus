Teacher Resources

Classroom‑ready materials for teaching Strides Between Us

About This Guide

This resource package is designed for educators teaching Strides Between Us in Grades 8–10. It includes themes, seminar questions, curriculum alignment, and downloadable materials.

Major Themes

  • Homelessness & hidden struggles
  • Mentorship & caring adults
  • Identity & lost potential
  • Trauma, shame & silence
  • Animal connection & emotional healing

Seminar / Discussion Questions

  • Why do young people hide their struggles?
  • What makes a mentor truly life‑changing?
  • How does identity shift after loss or injury?
  • Why is homelessness often invisible?
  • Where does hope come from when everything falls apart?

Curriculum Alignment (Ontario)

This novel aligns with expectations in:

  • Grade 8–10 English: Reading, Critical Thinking, Making Connections
  • Health & Well‑Being: Mental Health, Identity, Resilience
  • Equity & Inclusion: Understanding socio‑economic barriers
