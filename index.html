About the Novel

Strides Between Us follows Jake, a promising young runner whose life collapses after a devastating injury during his senior year. Lost, isolated, and unable to cope, he drifts into years of homelessness, severed from the people who loved him most.

Through memory, grief, and the quiet power of human connection, Jake slowly rebuilds himself, confronting the past he ran from and the mentor he failed to say goodbye to.

It is a novel about resilience, identity, second chances, and the belief that even after losing everything, a person can still find their way back.
